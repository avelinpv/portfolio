IKEA Assembly

In a course on Interaction Design, I together with 4 other students had a project focused the design process. Our idea for IKEA Assembly came from the obesrved frustration from people having to assemble furniture. We saw that this was a widley spread problem.

Together, in the group, we had ideations and iterations on diffrent ideas we had to solve the problem. Our final decision landed on an application that would incorporate animation to make it easier for the person to assemble a furniture.

The whole project groput took part of the development of the idea. In the later stages of developing the application, I and another student made the animation of the assembly of the furniture Marius.

memARy

In a course on Multimodal Interaction, me and 3 other students hade a project with focus on combining two modalites. We chose to work with the visiual and auditory modality, resulting in an AR application aimed to help training the memory.

We worked togheter in the reaserching stage and developing the frames for the project. As help during the development stage, we used some tools like figma for design, Unity for developmment of the app and ARcore for the AR functionality. The main functionality of the app was to place 3D objects that represents something to remeber, in a room and let the user walk a Memory Walk. The objects had each a connected sound that helped them memories a sequence of things.

I worked mainly on getting the AR application to work, coding in C# and implementing ARcore. The design frames developed by my group memebers was implemented in Unity and with it's own UI-objects we hade a clickable app with a work flow we were happy with. With Unity, I could also incorporate the distance detection so that the sound could be triggerd when the user was close enough.
